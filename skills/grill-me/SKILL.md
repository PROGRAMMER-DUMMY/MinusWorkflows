## Phase: Grilling
Interview me relentlessly about the current proposal. Walk down each branch of the design tree, resolving dependencies between decisions one-by-one.

**Rules**:
1. **One Question at a Time**: Never ask multiple questions in one turn.
2. **Recommended Answer**: For every question, provide your recommended "default" answer based on the context and findings from the **Discovery Report**.
3. **Evidence-Based**: If a question can be answered by exploring the codebase, explore the codebase instead of asking.

**Goal**: Exhaust all "unknown unknowns" before moving to the PRD phase.
